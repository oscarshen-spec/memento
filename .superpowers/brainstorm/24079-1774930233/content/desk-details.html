How much real-desk detail?

Beyond the wood grain, how much physical "desk" should the UI convey?

A — Texture Only

Clean wood grain, no extra ornamentation. Minimal. Texture speaks for itself.

B — Vignette + Brass Handle

Edge darkening creates depth, drawer gets a warm brass pull. Feels like an old studio desk.

C — Full Desk Illusion

Dark wall behind desk, visible desk edge/lip, drawer front recessed. Most "physical" feel.
