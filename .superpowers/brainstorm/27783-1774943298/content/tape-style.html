What kind of tape should it look like?

This affects both the toolbar icon and how the tape renders on the scrapbook page

A — Washi Tape

Semi-transparent, colorful, decorative. Has a slight texture and jagged torn ends. Fits the analog craft aesthetic perfectly.

B — Clear Scotch Tape

Transparent with a subtle glossy sheen and light blue tint. Realistic, minimal. Shows through to what's underneath.

C — Masking Tape

Cream/beige matte finish, slightly textured paper surface. Wide, opaque. Authentic vintage craft feel.
