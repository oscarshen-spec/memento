How does the tape follow your swipe?

This shapes the core interaction — does the tape go straight or follow the finger?

A — Straight strip, any angle

Tape is always a clean straight strip. The angle is set by the direction you begin swiping. Clean, predictable, authentic to how tape rolls dispense.

B — Free-form path, follows finger

Tape wraps exactly where you drag, curves and all. More expressive and playful — like pulling tape off a roll and sticking it anywhere.
